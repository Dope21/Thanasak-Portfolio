Timeline Checking Website2022-

Due to the covid-19 in Thailand, people need to write their names on the paper before entering the building.

Me and my friend wanted to build an ID card scanner to retrieve the information of the people who entered instead of writing on paper and uploading that infomation onto this website so people can check their timeline afterward.

Stack HTML, SCSS, jQuery, PHP, mySQL (for the website)
Source https://github.com/Dope21/covid-timeline
